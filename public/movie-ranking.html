My top 4 movies of all-time.

"The Exorcist" (Horror)

The film features Ellen Burstyn, Max von Sydow, Jason Miller, and Linda Blair.
This is one of the greatest horror films ever made. It has a significant influence on popular culture.
The cultura conversation around the movie was superb, and the sound engineering syntillating!

"The Good, the Bad, and the Ugly" (Western)

This movie is a 1966 Italian epic Spaghetti western film directed by Sergio Leone and starring Clint Eastwood as "the Good", Lee Van Cleef as "the Bad", and Eli Wallach as "the Ugly".
This is perhaps the greatest of the the Spaghetti westerns. The epic film features a compelling story, memorable performance, breathtaking lanscape, and a haunting score of critics!

"The Untouchables" (Gangster)

This movie is an unusual gangster movie in with the primary focus on the police, watching as they attempt to bring down the city's big bad. Released in 1987, this movie was directed by Brian De Palma.
The film is loosely based on the book of the same title (1957) and the real-life events it is based on, but most of its plot is fictionalized.

"A.I. Artificiala Intellience" (Sci-Fi)

This movie (or simply AI) is a 2001 American science fiction (Sci-Fi) film directed by Steve Spieberg.
It is based on the 1969 short story, "Supertoys Last All Summer Long" by Brian Aldiss. It has so many themes, ideas, feelings, and emotions.
The movie is one of the most thrilling Sci-Fi movies of all time. It has the heart, mind, and body. Like so many movies with the role of a child, this one showslove and emotion. It moves one to tears as David, the main character, craves for his mother till the end where his wait was all worth it.
A perfect family movie.
A perfect movie based on the future.
A perfect movie for technology lovers!
